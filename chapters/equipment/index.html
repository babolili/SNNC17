Let’s start with your equipment. Chances are, you will have more than you really need.

Kitchen gadgetry is sometimes pretty exciting stuff, but don’t get carried away. Too many tools can be paralysing. If strapped for cash, there are only a few things that you REALLY need, and others that only make life a little easier.

Then there are some tools that only seem to make life easier until you realize you have to wash them. A basic kit for kitchen survival will take you a long way.
