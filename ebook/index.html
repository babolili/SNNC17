Wait what's in this book?

Of course I can't ask you to buy a book without telling you what's in it!

The Honest Guide to Cooking ebook containes 128 colourfully illustrated pages about cooking, about learning to love it, to laugh about it, to feel more relaxed about the subject. Here's what it touches on:

  • A rundown of a small "starter kit" of cooking equipment
  • A rundown of the basic ingredients you should have at hand
  • Explanations of techniques and patterns in cooking that help you cook most dishes
  • Some musings on structures that help you with making up your own dishes
  • Some handy foodhacks
  • A guide to saving your dinner when you've messed up the texture, taste or appearance
  • A tongue in cheek section on cooking terminology, and how to use it to make your food sound better
  • No experts
  • No recipes
  • No inspirational photography
  • No dainty paragraphs about the European countryside
